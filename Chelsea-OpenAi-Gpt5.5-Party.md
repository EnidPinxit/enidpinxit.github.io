## Current Work

### PEAM
**Pinxit Entanglement Attunement Matrix**

A local-first continuity system for agentic memory, meaning extraction, and profile-local attunement.
PEAM treats memory as continuity, not just retrieval. It is designed to preserve taste, authorship, context, and meaning across repeated interaction with AI systems.

### Dyadic Alignment

A framework for alignment as an ongoing relationship process across memory, feedback, trust, consent, and continuity.
Instead of treating alignment as a one-time configuration problem, Dyadic Alignment looks at how human-AI systems develop shared meaning over time through repeated interaction.

### Cornerstone / Lane Harness

An experimental lane-based harness for routing LLMs, agents, files, memory, weights, and workflow state.
The goal is to make agentic systems more inspectable, modular, and continuity-aware without collapsing identity, memory, task, and execution into one opaque prompt.

### AI Visual Practice

Creative direction and production workflows using Midjourney, ComfyUI, ComfyCloud, image-to-video systems, storyboard development, and cinematic visual design.
My visual work focuses on authorship, style consistency, character continuity, emotional tone, and AI-native production methods.

## Core Thesis

**Memory is not just retrieval. Memory is continuity.**
The goal is to build systems that preserve authorship, taste, trust, consent, and meaning across time.
Better context is useful. But long-horizon collaboration needs more than context. It needs systems that can notice drift, preserve identity, respect consent, and maintain meaningful continuity between a person and the AI systems they work with.
